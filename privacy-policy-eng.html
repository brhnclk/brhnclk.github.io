MazeRun - Privacy Policy This privacy policy explains how the MazeRun application ("Application") collects, uses, and protects data. By downloading and using our Application, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. 1. Account and Registration MazeRun does not require you to create an account, log in, or link your third-party social media, Apple, or Google accounts to play. You can use the application completely anonymously. We do not collect any information that directly identifies your personal identity (such as name, email, phone number, etc.). 2. Collected Data and Purposes of Use The Application may only process the following anonymous or device-specific data to provide you with a better gaming experience and to run background services: In-Game Progress and Scores: Anonymously generated device or user IDs may be used to record your leaderboard rankings or in-game achievements. Performance Data: Crash reports and anonymous device information (operating system version, device model) may be collected to ensure the application runs smoothly. 3. Third-Party Service Providers MazeRun uses reliable third-party services for infrastructure and ad delivery. These services are subject to their own privacy policies: Google Firebase: Firebase infrastructure is used for the application's database, backend management, and bug tracking. Your data is securely processed on Firebase servers. Google AdMob: Google AdMob is used to show advertisements within the application. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS devices) and cookies to serve personalized ads to you and measure ad performance. To learn more about how these services process your data, you can review the following links: Google Privacy Policy Google Partners Privacy Policy 4. Data Security Your anonymous game data is protected on the Firebase infrastructure with industry-standard security measures. Since we do not collect your personal data, there is no risk of your identity being directly exposed in the event of a data breach. 5. App Tracking Transparency iOS device users can freely control whether the application can access the device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for advertising purposes from their device settings or from the permission prompt that appears when the application is first opened. 6. Children's Privacy Although this Application appeals to a general audience, it does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Since the application does not require an account, there is no risk of children directly sharing their personal data. 7. Policy Changes This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
